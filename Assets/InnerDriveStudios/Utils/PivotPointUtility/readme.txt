Description:
- Simply utility to move a hierarchy root to the bottom center position relative to its children, 
  while keeping the children's world position in place.
  Basically the same as Unity's "Center On Children" menu option, but then "Center Bottom Pivot On Children"

Example:
- Open the DemoScene, select the Ducks, Select "Center On Children" from the GameObject menu and then with only
  one duck selected at a time, choose "Utilities/Inner Drive Studios/Bottom Center Pivot"
