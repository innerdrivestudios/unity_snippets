Description:
- Simple utility to replace objects in your scene with prefabs.

What is different from the default "Drag another prefab onto this GameObject's prefab slot to swap it out" is that this utility allows you to:
- swap out NON prefab gameobjects for prefabs 
- randomize the prefab replacement selection

Example:
- Open the PrefabReplacerDemo
- Select all the *Tile objects
- Open Utilities/Inner Drive Studios/Prefab Replacer and configure it (check the tooltips in the dialog)
- Click 'Replace' (a couple of times)
